Privacy Policy for JuiceLab

Last Updated: June 3, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how JuiceLab ("the app", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your data when you use the JuiceLab mobile application and our associated services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Temporary Anonymous Identifiers (Guest Users): To allow you to use the app without registering, we create an anonymous guest session protected with a secure JSON Web Token (JWT). This allows you to build and save recipes locally on your device.
  • Account and Profile Data: If you choose to register a permanent account (e.g., via password login or Sign in with Apple), we store your email address and a securely encrypted version of your password. If you upgrade from a guest account, we automatically merge your temporary guest data into your new permanent profile.
  • User Configurations and Settings: We store your personalized overrides directly in our secure database (Azure SQL), including custom ingredient yields, ingredient prices (internally stored in USD), category memberships, cleaning difficulties, and customized recipe yields.
  • Recipes and Brewing History: We store the custom recipes you create, alongside historical snapshots of your active and completed juicing rounds (batches).
  • Technical Logs: We anonymously collect error logs and system performance data (via Azure Log Analytics) to help us troubleshoot technical issues and maintain app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to deliver and improve the functional features of the app, including calculating nutrition profiles, estimating costs per glass using your custom price overrides, and synchronizing your saved recipes across multiple devices when logged in.

A Note on Nutritional Data: All nutrition calculations and vitamin profiles in JuiceLab are provided purely as estimates for informational purposes. The app does not track medical health records and does not share your data with external medical platforms (such as Apple Health).

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We never sell your personal information. However, to deliver specific app features, we coordinate with the following secure providers:

  • AI-Generated Recipes ("Empty the Fridge"): When using AI features, we pass the text list of selected ingredients and target volume to our AI processing providers (such as Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini). We never transmit your name, email, or other personal data to these providers.
  • Payments and Subscriptions (Apple StoreKit): All purchases are managed exclusively by Apple (StoreKit). JuiceLab never views or stores your credit card details. Apple transmits only an anonymized, signed transaction token (OriginalTransactionId) to verify your Premium entitlement.

4. Data Security

Your data is housed securely on cloud architecture located in Europe (Microsoft Azure). All communication between the mobile application and our API infrastructure is strictly encrypted using HTTPS protocols.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

In strict compliance with Apple’s app guidelines, you hold the absolute right to delete your user account permanently. You can initiate a complete account erasure directly from the Profile screen within the app. Upon deletion, your data is removed permanently from our active databases.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data deletion rights, you can reach out to us using the contact form endpoint in the application or via our support email.
